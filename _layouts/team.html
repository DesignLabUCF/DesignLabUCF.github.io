Team

The SENSEable Design Lab group is part of the Institute for Simulation and Training at the University of Central Florida. It is directed by Dr. Joseph T. Kider Jr. in the School for Modeling, Simulation, and Training and a secondary appointment in Computer Science. Researchers in the SENSEable Design Lab are anti-disciplinary - meaning they come from interdisciplinary backgrounds and purse transdisiplinary research. Our lab and research incorporates students from the bachelor, Masters, and Ph.D. level.
